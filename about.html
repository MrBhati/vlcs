Who We Are

Serving Maharashtra
For Over 25 Years

We Lift the Future of Mumbai

Established in the late 1990s in Kalamboli, Navi Mumbai, Vijay Laxmi Crane Service began with a single pick & carry hydra crane. Over the past 2.5 decades, through sheer commitment to safety and customer service, we have expanded our fleet to include heavy-duty all-terrain mobile cranes up to 700T, boom lifts, forklifts, and excavators.

"Safety is not just a protocol; it's our license to operate. We measure our success by the zero-accident record we maintain on every industrial site."

We work closely with structural engineers, project managers, and logistics coordinators to provide bespoke rigging and engineered lifting schemes. Whether it's the erection of refinery columns in Thane, metro station builds in central Mumbai, or warehousing lifts in Bhiwandi, we have the machinery and expertise to execute safely and on schedule.

Our Foundation

Our Core Values

01. Uncompromising Safety

We enforce rigorous testing, weekly machine maintenance, and continuous safety training for operators. Every single crane in our fleet is certified and fitted with modern Safe Load Indicator (SLI) systems.

02. absolute reliability

We understand that project delays are expensive. Our logistics coordination is meticulous, ensuring cranes arrive on-site pre-rigged and ready to perform at the designated time, backed by standby mechanics.

03. Professional Expertise

Our operator and rigging team consists of seasoned veterans holding government-approved heavy vehicle and safety certifications. We bring deep know-how to complex, multi-crane tandem lifts.

Vijay Laxmi Crane Service is Mumbai's premier provider of heavy lifting solutions, crane rentals, and rigging services. We serve clients across Maharashtra with safety, reliability, and precision.
